# Site Map

## Purpose

This document defines the planned website structure before implementation. It does not define final copy, visual design, technology stack, routing implementation, or build approach.

## Primary pages

## Home

Purpose: Explain who Northdown Computers AI Systems & Automation serves, what it does, why it matters, and how visitors can start a practical conversation.

Role in journey: Visitor -> Interest -> Discovery.

## Services

Purpose: Present the service catalog clearly without pricing, stack choices, or implementation promises.

Primary service areas:

- AI Systems Audits.
- Automation Workflows.
- Internal Tools.
- Knowledge Systems.
- Agentic Engineering.
- Operational AI Infrastructure.

Role in journey: Interest -> Discovery -> Qualification.

## About

Purpose: Build trust by explaining the business orientation, working principles, operational discipline, and human oversight approach.

Role in journey: Visitor -> Interest -> Trust.

## Process

Purpose: Explain how work moves from first contact through discovery, audit, proposal, delivery, validation, handoff, and optimisation.

Role in journey: Interest -> Discovery -> Qualification.

## Contact

Purpose: Convert qualified interest into a structured enquiry or client intake path.

Role in journey: Qualification -> Contact -> Client intake.

## Resources

Purpose: Provide practical educational material, source-led notes, FAQs, and guidance that help visitors understand operational AI and automation before contacting Northdown.

Role in journey: Visitor -> Interest -> Discovery.

## Privacy

Purpose: Explain privacy, data handling, contact-form expectations, and boundaries around sensitive information.

Role in journey: Trust support across all stages.

## Navigation principles

- Keep the primary navigation simple.
- Make services, process, and contact easy to find.
- Avoid burying trust signals in long pages only.
- Do not add pages for pricing, case studies, integrations, stack, or compliance until approved source material exists.

## Future page candidates

These require approval before creation:

- Case Studies.
- FAQs.
- Individual service pages.
- Client portal or operating base.
- Blog or knowledge base.
- Legal pages beyond Privacy.
