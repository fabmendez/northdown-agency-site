# Homepage Brief

## Page goal

Explain what Northdown Computers AI Systems & Automation does, who it helps, and how a business can start a practical conversation without needing to understand AI terminology.

## Primary message

Northdown Computers AI Systems & Automation builds practical AI systems, automation workflows, internal tools, and operational AI infrastructure for small and mid-sized businesses.

## Audience

Business owners, managers, and operators who are dealing with repeated admin, disconnected tools, unclear processes, or pressure to adopt AI sensibly.

## Suggested page structure

### Hero

Use the business name as the first strong signal. The supporting copy should explain the offer plainly.

Example direction:

- Heading: Northdown Computers AI Systems & Automation
- Supporting copy: Practical AI systems, automation workflows, internal tools, and operational AI infrastructure for small and mid-sized businesses.
- Primary action: Book an operational review
- Secondary action: See what can be automated

### Problem section

Show recognizable operational problems:

- Repeated admin across email, spreadsheets, and shared folders.
- Important tasks depending on memory or manual follow-up.
- Business information spread across too many places.
- Interest in AI without a clear, safe starting point.

### Offer section

Explain the core services:

- AI systems and agentic workflows.
- Business process automation.
- Internal tools and dashboards.
- Operational AI infrastructure.
- Workflow review and improvement.

### Service ladder section

Present a simple path:

1. Review the operation.
2. Choose the first workflow.
3. Build the tool or automation.
4. Document and validate it.
5. Improve it over time.

### Trust and safety section

Make the working principles clear:

- Human approval for important decisions.
- Least-privilege access.
- No unnecessary dependencies.
- Clear documentation and handoff.
- Practical systems before hype.

### Call to action

Invite a focused first conversation around one workflow or operational bottleneck. Avoid promising a full transformation before discovery.

## Copy rules

- Use practical business language.
- Avoid exaggerated AI claims.
- Avoid naming specific platforms until approved.
- Do not mention pricing until approved.
- Do not claim compliance coverage until validated.
- Keep the site focused on operational improvement and controlled adoption.
