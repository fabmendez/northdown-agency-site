# Services

## Category

Operational Systems Engineering

Northdown is an operations engineering company that uses AI where appropriate.

Northdown is not:

- An AI agency.
- A generic automation company.
- A prompt consultancy.

## Hero

### Services That Make Your Business Easier To Run

Northdown designs and builds operational systems that reduce repeated work, retain business knowledge, and improve consistency as you grow.

## Operational Problems We Solve

- Repeated manual work.
- Bottlenecks between people, tools, and processes.
- Information trapped in inboxes or spreadsheets.
- Undocumented workflows.
- Inconsistent delivery.
- Duplicated admin.
- Teams depending on memory instead of systems.

## Service Categories

### Operational AI Systems

AI-assisted systems designed around real operational workflows, not novelty demos.

### Workflow Automation

Automations that move information, trigger actions, reduce handoffs, and prevent repeated admin.

### Knowledge Systems

Internal systems that capture processes, decisions, client context, and operational know-how.

### Operational Infrastructure

Private, maintainable systems that support your operating model, boundaries, and growth.

## What Delivery Can Include

- Workflow mapping.
- System design.
- Automation buildout.
- Internal tools.
- Documentation systems.
- Knowledge capture.
- AI-assisted processes.
- Validation and handover.

## Good Fit

- Growing businesses with repeated operational work.
- Teams losing time to manual admin.
- Businesses where knowledge lives in people's heads.
- Owners who want clearer systems before adding more people.
- Teams that value practical delivery over hype.

## Poor Fit

- Businesses looking for AI gimmicks.
- Teams wanting fake metrics or instant transformation claims.
- Projects with no owner or decision-maker.
- Requests to bypass governance, privacy, or validation.
